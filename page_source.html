The Problem

Current fuel pricing solutions are outdated, expensive, and inaccurate. Here's what travelers really need:

Outdated Data

API data is 12 hrs to multiple days old. Fuel prices change hourly.

Expensive APIs

Real-time futures data costs money. Only commodity futures available.

Inaccurate Pricing

No spot prices. Futures don't match what you actually pay at the pump.

Limited Coverage

APIs miss regional variations and local gas station pricing.

The Real Issue:

Travelers are at the pump every day. They see real prices in real-time. But this invaluable data is never captured or shared. Existing solutions rely on outdated APIs instead of tapping into the knowledge of people actually buying fuel.

The Solution

Harness the power of your user base to create the most accurate fuel pricing network ever built.

Crowdsourced Data

Every app user contributes real-time fuel prices from actual gas stations they visit.

Accuracy They Can't Match

Spot prices from your network beat any paid API. Real data from real locations.

Competitive Advantage

This data is unique to your apps. No other platform has access to it.

Instant Updates

Price changes are reflected immediately across your entire network.

What Users Do:

  • ✓Confirm fuel prices when they fill up at any station
  • ✓Report availability (diesel out, premium available, etc.)
  • ✓See real market insights across your travel network
  • ✓Earn badges and rewards for participation

The network becomes more valuable the more people participate. This is how you build an unbeatable competitive advantage.
